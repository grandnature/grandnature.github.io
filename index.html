A Grandparent's Guide to Exploring Nature


A collection of activities to bring your grandchildren closer to Nature, and to you.


Children are naturally curious and what better outlet can there be for this curiosity than exploring the world around us? A love of Nature fostered during childhood is likely to last a lifetime. It is a gift which grandparents are often best placed to give. The activities collected here are not intended to be prescriptive - you should adapt them in any way you see fit to match your circumstances and the age and interests of your grandchildren.
