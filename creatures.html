Creatures


Living creatures are perhaps the most exciting aspect of the natural world and are always a source of fascination to children. Even if you live in a town or city there are plenty to be spotted and studied, especially birds and small invertebrates (insects, molluscs, crustaceans, worms, spiders etc.).

  • Start a Nature Journal - a place for children to draw and write about their encounters with Nature.
  • Look for Life Underneath logs and stones - young children will have fun just seeing what they can spot, older ones can approach it as an experiment.
